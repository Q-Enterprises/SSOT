- The local `pydantic` shim in `pydantic/__init__.py` was missing the `errors()` method on `ValidationError`.
- This caused `codex_validator.validate_payload` to crash when validation failed.
- Added `ValidationError.errors()` returning a list of dicts with a "msg" key.
- Implemented tests in `tests/test_codex_validator.py` to verify validation logic and the shim fix.
